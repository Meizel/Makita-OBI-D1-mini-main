## How to use the new features

### 1. Physical change (Critical)

The battery **EN** wire must be connected to **D5** with a 4.7k pull-up to 3.3V.

### 2. mDNS Access

You no longer need to find the IP. Just connect to the WiFi and enter:
**`http://makita.local`**

### 3. Cell Diagnostics

Use the history chart to detect cells that drop in voltage under load. If the **Dynamic HUD** turns red, the pack needs balancing or repair.

## How to use the tool

1. **Access**: Connect to the `Makita_OBI_ESP8266` WiFi.
2. **Browser**: Go to `http://makita.local`.
3. **Reading**: Connect a Makita battery and click "Read Info".
